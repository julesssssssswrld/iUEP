About iUEP

We believe that accessing your academic information shouldn't require a physical commute. Born from the need to modernize the University of Eastern Philippines' digital infrastructure, iUEP was designed to solve a simple problem: accessibility.

Previously, essential tasks like checking grades often meant physically visiting the CCS building. We have transformed that process by creating a cloud-native platform that puts the Student Portal in your pocket.

We also consolidate essential tools—class schedules, grade monitoring, and event calendars—into one seamless interface. Beyond academics, iUEP serves as the central nervous system for campus communication. By aggregating announcements from the Administration and Departmental Student Councils, we ensure you are always up to date, whether it's a class suspension or a campus festivity.
